Coaching Questions for the Conversation
  • When you think about your most impactful work this cycle — how do you know it had impact? What would you do differently to make that impact more visible?
    Why ask: helps surface the commercial framing gap in a non-threatening way. GRF-047 will likely describe impact in design terms; the follow-up is "how would you describe that in a way a CFO would care about?"
  • I've heard that you've been really helpful to some of your colleagues in developing their design practice this cycle. Is that something you do intentionally — and is it something you'd want to build on?
    Why ask: surfaces the mentoring behaviour GRF-047 doesn't credit themselves for. Don't reveal the source. The goal is for GRF-047 to recognise and own it.
  • You've said you want to move into design leadership and help shape Griffin's design direction rather than just deliver within it. What would need to be true for you to feel like you were on that path?
    Why ask: makes the aspiration concrete and actionable. Gives you information about what GRF-047 actually needs, rather than assuming.
  • If you were presenting a significant design decision to a senior commercial stakeholder tomorrow — how would you frame it? What would you want them to take away?
    Why ask: directly explores the commercial framing gap in a practical, forward-looking way. Useful for agreeing what the development work actually looks like.
  • Where do you feel you're being stretched most right now — and where could I do more to support that stretch?
    Why ask: gives GRF-047 agency and gives you information about what support would actually land. Keep this question genuinely open.

GRF-047's self-underestimation of mentoring contribution could be misread as absence of strength. Take care not to let the absence of self-credit reduce how this is weighted in your own assessment.
The leader rating without examples may reflect genuine strong assessment based on broader context — but it may also reflect advocacy bias or positive halo effect. Ground the conversation in specific behaviours and outcomes.
📋 Sensitivity notes
